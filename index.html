This is Vasco: a Musician a Programmer a Sound Engineer a Contact to have
Jack of all trades, master of.. some!

While in high-school, Vasco studied sciences and technology, with humble but steady scores. Shortly thereafter, he pursued a career of music and sound engineering, learning harmony, music theory and to play the piano, as well as fiddling with the control knobs and faders in a mixing table.

After concluding his studies on said subjects, Vasco realized he'd much prefer not to depend on its financial outcomes, fearing he might compromise his projects.

Hence, decided to seek to deepen knowledge in an area in which he was always been curious: programming! Deep down, what truly mesmerizes Vasco is the possibility of creating new things and achieving simple, yet virtuous solutions.
