Vasco Vilhena: a Musician a Sound Engineer a Programmer a Contact to have
Jack of all trades, master of.. some!

While in High School, Vasco studied science & technology, collecting humble but steady scores. Shortly after, he pursued a career in music and sound engineering, having learnt harmony, music theory and how to play the piano - as well as how to fiddle with the faders and control knobs of a mixing table.

After finishing his studies, Vasco realized he would like to deepen his knowledge in a different field. One he had always been curious about: programming!

Deep down, what mesmerizes Vasco is the possibility of creating new things and achieving simple yet virtuous solutions - whether it is by mixing notes and arpeggios or coding in C#!
